About

Julia Thummel is a student in her sophomore year at Washington University in St. Louis. Originally from Skokie, a suburb just north of Chicago, Julia is passionate about pizza and used to cold, windy winters- but that by no means makes her enjoy the winters in Saint Louis.
Being in the art school, Julia rarely has time for anything but design work. But in her spare time, she enjoys cooking, interior decoration, and growing plants.
